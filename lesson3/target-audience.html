Target Audience

The target audience for the page will include those who are looking for residence in the area, events, community opportunities, weather, and more. The City of Countryside is located in Cook County, Illinois. With a population of 6,420 (2020 census) Countryside is a close-knit community with many citizens being second and third generation residents of the City. The demographic is just over half female with the median age being 45. The average family size is 3.09 so our target has a small family they're considering when using this site. The median income for families is $74,750.
