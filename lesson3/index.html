Site Purpose

This website will help to promote and assist businesses in the area in and around Countryside, IL by providing opportunities for community involvement, consumer engagement, educational programming, local marketing, and more. There will be a place to see events, accomplishments, good news, upcoming information, and more. The website will join community together while providing information about what is currently happening around town. Also, this website will help business owners remain informed, connected and engaged, while also helping their businesses gain visibility in the community.
